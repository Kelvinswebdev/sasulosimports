About Sasulos Consult Limited

At Sasulos Consult Limited, we make hiring from Ghana seamless. As one of West Africa’s leading manpower recruitment agencies, we specialize in supplying vetted, trained, and dependable workers to companies across the Middle East—including Saudi Arabia, Dubai, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, and the UAE. Whether you need carpenters, electricians, hospitality staff, healthcare professionals, or general laborers, we deliver talent that’s ready to work and built to last.

Business engagement model

Sasulos Consult Ltd offers a transparent, flexible, and client-focused engagement model. We work closely with our partners to assess their specific needs and deliver tailored workforce solutions. Key highlights of our model include:

  • Fast deployment to Gulf countries and beyond
  • No commission charged to employers
  • Vetted and trained workers ready for immediate mobilization
  • End-to-end handling of travel and documentation
  • GLMIS-listed, ensuring full legal compliance and credibility

Our History | Core Values | Mission & Vision

Since our establishment in 2018, Sasulos Consult Limited has been a trusted partner for meeting international demands. We are committed to providing a highly personalized and tailored service. Our goal is to understand the specific needs and challenges of each client, and then provide the perfect talent to help them solve problems and achieve their business objectives.

Our vision is to empower young Ghanaians to achieve better career development abroad by leading the way in creating and delivering innovative workforce solutions. We strive to provide services that enable our clients, both companies and employees, to succeed in the ever-changing world of work.

Our mission is to provide heartfelt service and value to everyone we encounter. We're dedicated to delivering top-quality training programs so workers are well-equipped for their job placements.
